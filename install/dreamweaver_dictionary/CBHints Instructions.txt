STEP 1:

Copy and past hintCB.gif into the following directory:

[Drive]:\Program Files\Adobe\[DreamweaverInstallDir]\configuration\Shared\MM\Images

STEP 2:

In the CodeHints.xml configuration file located:

[Drive]:\Program Files\Adobe\[DreamweaverInstallDir]\configuration\CodeHints.xml

<menugroup MMString:name="codehints_6" id="CodeHints_Function_Arguments">
  <description>
  <MMString:loadString id="codehints_7" />
  </description>

</menugroup>
